Melissa provides tailored bookkeeping and financial support services to small businesses and nonprofit organizations, helping them achieve clarity, compliance, and measurable outcomes.

For many organizations, hiring a full-time financial professional isn't always feasible. That’s where Melissa comes in—as a trusted partner who offers both strategic insight and flexible support. Whether you're launching, stabilizing, or scaling, Melissa delivers the expertise you need at every stage of growth.

Explore a range of customizable services designed to meet your unique operational and financial needs.

Services provided include:

  • Monthly Bookkeeping:Ongoing financial management to keep your books accurate, organized, and up-to-date.
    • Bank and credit card reconciliations
    • Categorization of income and expenses
    • Monthly financial reports (Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, etc.)
    • General ledger maintenance
  • Catch up and Clean Up Services: Behind on your books? Melissa will bring everything up to date and correct errors to ensure clean, reliable financials.
    • Historical transaction cleanup
    • Reconciliation of past months or years
    • Correction of miscategorized entries
    • Preparation for tax filings or audits
  • Bank Reconciliation
  • Accounts Payable/Receivable
  • Payroll Services: Accurate payroll processing and compliance support using your preferred system.
    • Payroll setup and processing (iSolved, ADP, Paylocity, etc.)
    • Payroll journal entries and reconciliations
    • Contractor payments and 1099 tracking
    • Employee time tracking integration
  • Financial Reports: Gain deeper understanding of your finances with tailored reporting and analysis.
    • Custom monthly or quarterly reporting
    • Budget-to-actual comparisons
    • Cash flow tracking
    • Grant and program-specific tracking (for nonprofits)
  • Software Setup & Training: Starting fresh or improving your current setup? Melissa can help you build a system that works.
    • QuickBooks Online setup and training
    • Chart of accounts customization
    • Integration with tools like Bill.com, Gusto, and more
    • Establish complete workflow and process systems or risk assessment of current systems for improvements.
