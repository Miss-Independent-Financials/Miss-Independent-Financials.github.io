Melissa Faux

Melissa Faux brings over 25 years of experience in business management, with a strong foundation in operations, finance, and organizational leadership. Her career began in retail operations, where she successfully managed high-performing teams for nationally recognized brands such as Victoria’s Secret and Starbucks.

Driven by a passion for business efficiency and financial clarity, Melissa earned her MBA with a concentration in Finance and Organizational Management. Since then, she has provided comprehensive financial services to a diverse client base—including small businesses, retail operations, and mid-sized to large nonprofit organizations.

Melissa is certified in QuickBooks Online and has extensive experience with payroll platforms such as iSolved, ADP, and Paylocity. She is also well-versed in financial management tools like Bill.com, ensuring streamlined and compliant processes for every client she serves.
