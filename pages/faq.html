FAQs

What types of businesses do you work with?

I specialize in working with small businesses, freelancers, and solo proprietors across a variety of industries including retail, non-profits, and creative services. Whether you're just starting out or have been in business for years, I can tailor my services to fit your needs.

Do you offer virtual/remote bookkeeping services?

Yes! I work with clients remotely from Massachusetts to South Florida. All communication, document sharing, and reporting can be done securely online — making the process simple and stress-free for you.

What bookkeeping software do you use?

I primarily work with QuickBooks Online, but experienced with Blackbaud products as well. Working with a consultant means I am expecting to familiarize myself with the platforms that you have invested in and make recommendations for others that will meet your business needs. I can also assist with setup and training if you're new to bookkeeping software.

How do you ensure my financial data stays secure?

Security is a top priority. I use secure, encrypted file-sharing tools and follow best practices to keep your data confidential and protected. I’m also happy to sign a confidentiality agreement if needed.

How do I get started?

Getting started is easy! Simply [fill out the contact form](#) or [book a free consultation](#) and we’ll set up a quick discovery call to talk about your business needs. From there, I’ll provide a personalized quote and next steps.

Do you offer one-time or catch-up bookkeeping?

Absolutely. Whether you’re behind on your books or just need help preparing for tax season, I offer catch-up and clean-up services to get you back on track.

How much do your services cost?

Pricing depends on the size of your business and the complexity of your needs. I offer custom packages to ensure you're only paying for what you need. I’m happy to provide a free quote after a short consultation.

Can you work with my accountant or tax preparer?

Yes, collaboration is key! I’m happy to work directly with your accountant to ensure everything is accurate and ready for tax time.

How often will I hear from you?

I’ll be in touch regularly depending on your service package — usually monthly or weekly. I also keep lines of communication open for any questions or support you may need.

Do you provide financial reporting?

Yes! I provide easy-to-understand financial reports such as profit & loss, balance sheets, and cash flow statements so you can see exactly how your business is doing.
